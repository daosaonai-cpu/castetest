The Concept: K-Means Clustering

Unlike Linear Regression, K-Means is an Unsupervised Learning algorithm. We don't give it "answers" (like predicting a price). Instead, we give it raw data and ask it to find underlying patterns or groups.

1. Initialize

Pick 'K' random points to be the starting center points (centroids) for our segments.

2. Assign

Calculate the distance from every customer to every centroid. Assign the customer to the closest one.

3. Update

Move the centroid to the exact average (mean) location of all the customers assigned to it. Repeat!
